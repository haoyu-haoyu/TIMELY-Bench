# Inclusion and Exclusion Criteria

Updated: 2026-02-07

## Inclusion Criteria

1. Study uses clinical EHR data in ICU/inpatient context.
2. Study combines both:
   - structured physiological/clinical time-series, and
   - unstructured clinical text.
3. Study reports at least one prediction task (mortality/LOS/readmission or equivalent).
4. Study reports quantitative evaluation (AUROC/AUPRC or equivalent).
5. Full text is accessible.

## Exclusion Criteria

1. Unimodal-only studies (time-series only or text only) unless used as benchmark baseline context.
2. Non-clinical datasets or purely simulation papers.
3. No predictive evaluation (methods-only without reported metrics).
4. Duplicate publication of same experiment (keep most complete version).
5. Non-English full text where translation is unavailable.

## Screening Decision Labels
